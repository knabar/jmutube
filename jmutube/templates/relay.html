Camtasia Relay

Camtasia Relay allows you to make live recordings of your presentations that are automatically converted and uploaded to JMUtube. At present, you must request a Relay account before you can submit your presentations to JMUtube. Contact Grover Saunders by email at saundebn@jmu.edu to request an account.

Click the appropriate link below to download the recording software for your computer. The Portable Recorder can be installed onto a USB flash drive for use in situations where the recording software can't be installed.
