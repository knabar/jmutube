Welcome to JMUtube

If you're reading this then you've found your way to JMUtube, the CIT's new site for the faculty and staff of JMU to store and deliver media for the JMU community. Currently the site is limited to just video, but stay tuned for many updates in the near future that will add support for audio, images, and even Adobe Presenter presentations.

Hope you enjoy the site.

Presentation Support Added • Dec 4th, 2008

Presentation support has been added to JMUtube. This will allow you to store and deploy Camtasia, Adobe Captivate, or Adobe Presenter presentations. Just zip your presentation into a single .zip file and upload via the Upload tab. In fact, you can actually upload just about any website as long as there's a single .html file in the root directory or one named index.html.
